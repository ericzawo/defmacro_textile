There is a plethora of information on branding, both on the internet and in print. Most of it is presented as a collection of barely related laws losely based on historical examples. Unfortunately this isn't very useful in practice. A hundred contradicting laws of branding are difficult to remember and difficult to apply. They don't present a coherent model of the world, are next to impossible to internalize, and carry little actionable advice.
